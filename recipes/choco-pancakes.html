Chocolate Pancakes

Chocolate pancakes are a delicious variation of traditional pancakes that incorporate cocoa powder or melted chocolate into the batter, giving them a rich and indulgent flavor. These delectable treats are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even as a special dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup milk (you can use regular milk or buttermilk for a tangier taste)
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips (optional, but adds extra chocolatey goodness)

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until well combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg, then add in the milk, melted butter or oil, and vanilla extract (if using). Mix until everything is well blended.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Stir gently with a spatula or whisk until just combined. If you're adding chocolate chips, fold them into the batter at this stage.
  4. Let the batter sit for a couple of minutes while you preheat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Lightly grease the cooking surface with butter or oil.
  5. Once the griddle/skillet is hot, ladle the pancake batter onto it, using about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles start to form on the surface, and the edges appear slightly cooked.
  6. Carefully flip the pancakes and cook the other side until it's golden brown.
  7. Transfer the cooked pancakes to a plate, and keep them warm in a preheated oven (set to low heat) while you cook the remaining batter.
  8. Serve the chocolate pancakes with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, whipped cream, chocolate sauce, fresh berries, sliced bananas, or chopped nuts. Enjoy your delicious chocolatey treat!
